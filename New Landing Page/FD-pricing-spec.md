# Fórmula Dinero — Pricing & Offering Spec
*A stage-based membership model built on the Estadios Económicos*

---

## 1. Core principle

Two axes, each doing exactly one job:

- **Estadio decides what you get.** The offering is built around where the member sits on their financial journey, not around a calendar.
- **Commitment length decides what you pay.** Monthly for accessibility, annual for the discount and the long-term lock-in.

Two rules flow from this:

1. **The Radiografía routes every member to one recommended plan** — so no one faces a confusing three-column menu. The diagnostic does the choosing.
2. **Every plan is named for the Estadio it takes you *to*, never the one you're stuck in.** Nobody buys the "Survival plan."

The model has three rungs: a **one-time tripwire** (Radiografía), a **recurring core** (Estabilidad), and a **recurring premium** (Crecimiento).

---

## 2. Foundation — the four Estadios Económicos

| # | Estadio | The member's reality | Served by | Launch status |
|---|---------|----------------------|-----------|---------------|
| 1 | **Supervivencia** | Covers the basics, no margin | *Estabilidad* plan | Target |
| 2 | **Estabilidad** | Emergency fund in construction | *Estabilidad* plan | Target — the bulk |
| 3 | **Crecimiento** | Consistent saving, first investments | *Crecimiento* plan | Target |
| 4 | **Libertad** | Passive income covers expenses | Future tier | Visible horizon |

Stages 1 and 2 share a single plan because their needs and toolset are nearly identical; forcing two tiers there would add price friction for the people least able to absorb it. Stage 3 gets its own plan because the tools genuinely change.

## 4. Level detail

### Mi Radiografía — one-time payment
The hook. A small one-time charge unlocks the member's full diagnosis.

**What you get**
- Full financial score plus the four sub-scores: Ahorro, Deudas, Inversión, Flujo
- Estadio Económico placement — *"here is where you are, and why"*
- Top prioritized áreas de oportunidad
- A **preview** of the Hoja de Ruta (full version locked)
- A few FD IA questions about your results, then locked

**Its job is not profit.** It converts a cold visitor into a paying customer, qualifies intent, captures a payment method, and routes the member to the right plan. **The fee is credited toward the first subscription payment.**

*Directional price: ~$9–19 one-time.*

### Estabilidad — subscription · serves Supervivencia + Estabilidad
The reality: little or no margin, an unfinished emergency fund, often high-interest debt. The whole job is **control and a first buffer.**

**What you get**
- The full **Hoja de Ruta** tuned to this stage: find margin, build the emergency fund, consolidate and kill bad debt, automate savings
- **Cada Menudo** budgeting app — full spending, income, and goal tracking
- **FD IA ilimitado**, scoped to foundation needs: budgeting, spending, debt payoff, "can I afford this," emergency-fund pacing, accountability nudges. *This is the member's coach at this level.*
- Foundational **calculadoras**: debt payoff, emergency fund, savings
- The **fundamentals course track** (money basics, budgeting, debt)
- Progress tracking — watch the score climb by re-running the Radiografía
- Human coaching available **à la carte** for the motivated

**Outcome it's built to produce:** full buffer + debt under control + consistent savings = the member crosses into Crecimiento.

*Directional price: ~$9–15/mo, or ~$135/yr (the repositioned $135 — now an accessible annual price, ~$11/mo).*

### Crecimiento — subscription · serves the Crecimiento stage
The reality: a surplus exists and debt is handled. The question shifts from "how do I survive" to "how do I grow."

**What you get — everything in Estabilidad, plus:**
- The **Hoja de Ruta** shifts to growth: optimize surplus, first investments, build net worth
- **Patrimonio Neto** (net-worth) tracking, front and center
- Advanced **calculadoras**: compound interest, investing, retiro
- The **advanced / investing course track**
- **1:1 certified coach — one session per quarter** (strategic review, not remedial)
- **FD IA opens up**: proactive monitoring (flags an unused subscription on its own), investment and net-worth analysis, scenario and projection modeling, optimization — plugged into the member's patrimonio data

**Outcome it's built to produce:** consistent investing and rising net worth, building toward Libertad.

*Directional price: ~$29–45/mo, or annual with the "mayor compromiso = mayor ahorro" discount.*

### Libertad — the visible horizon (not built at launch)
Where passive income covers expenses. Not served yet — but **shown on the ladder on purpose.** It tells every member there's a floor above them, which is the narrative that keeps people subscribed and climbing. Later it becomes higher-touch advisory, likely a different model.

## 5. The AI capability ladder

FD IA is gated by **depth and proactivity, never on/off.** This works because the AI's usefulness is already stage-dependent — a Supervivencia member can't ask it to analyze a portfolio they don't have, so withholding the advanced layer never feels punitive.

| Level | What FD IA does |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Radiografía** | A taste — a few questions about your results, then locked. Fine to gate hard; it's the hook. |
| **Estabilidad** | Unlimited, scoped to the stage: budgeting, spending, debt, affordability, accountability. Stays open — it's the coach for the people who can't afford a human. |
| **Crecimiento** | Opens up: proactive monitoring, investment & net-worth analysis, scenario modeling, optimization, plugged into patrimonio data. |

The line across levels is **"how deep and how proactive,"** not "AI vs. no AI." This gives a real upgrade pull *and* controls cost on heavy AI use, without gutting the tier that drives the funnel.

---

## 6. Coaching model

- **Estabilidad:** no human coaching bundled; available à la carte. FD IA carries daily accountability.
- **Crecimiento:** one 1:1 session per quarter (4/year).

The division of labor: **the human coach owns strategy, the AI owns execution.** A quarterly check-in is plenty for "here's where you are, here's the next quarter's focus," while FD IA handles questions and accountability in between. Four human touchpoints a year keeps coaching margin healthy at the Crecimiento price — human coaches, the one expensive and unscalable resource, are concentrated at the single stage that can pay for them.

---

## 7. Payment & conversion mechanics

- **Radiografía:** one-time charge; fee credited toward the first subscription payment.
- **Both subscriptions:** offered monthly (cancel anytime) **and** annual (discounted). Monthly converts cash-strapped stage-1 members; annual captures the long-term commitment and the discount.
- **Present the upgrade in the same flow, at the moment of motivation** — the instant the member sees they're stuck in Estabilidad, not in a follow-up email days later.
- **The progression itself is the upsell.** The product already detects when a member crosses from Estabilidad into Crecimiento — that crossing is the earned, emotionally powerful upgrade trigger: *"Felicidades, llegaste a Crecimiento. Esto es lo que sigue."*

---

## 8. Value anchor

A one-time professional financial review runs roughly **$1,000–$3,000** (US median ~$3,000), and traditional planners generally won't even serve a Supervivencia/Estabilidad client. That gap is the wedge: FD serves people the planners ignore.

Use the anchor to sell **value** — *"a real financial review costs thousands; FD gives you a clear, professional picture for a fraction"* — but **do not price to it.** The stage-1–2 member's constraint is cash, not perceived value. The anchor justifies the offering; accessibility sets the price.
